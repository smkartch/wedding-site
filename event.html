Our Vision

We're so excited to celebrate with you! We see this day not only as a chance to commemorate the joining of two families, but also as moment to show appreciation for all of you! Neither of us would be here without the support each of you has shown us along the way. We've been blessed by your friendships, guidance, listening ears, encouragement, laughter, and love so freely given. They say it takes a village to raise a person, and as we looked over all the invitations for this event, we were overcome with gratitude for the villages that raised us. Our hope is that this wedding feels like a small way to give back to the people we love most, and a chance for you to simply come, relax, and be celebrated too.
